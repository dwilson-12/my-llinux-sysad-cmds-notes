NIC = Network Interface Card
Example:
	$ ethtool enp0s3

Other NICs
	lo	= The loopback device is a special interface that your computer uses to communicate with itself.
	     	  If is used to mainly for diagnostics and troubleshooting, and to connect to servers running
	     	  on the local machine.

	virb0	= The virb0, or "Virtual bridge 0" interface is used for NAT (Network Address Transalation)
		  Virtual env sometimes use it to connect to the outside network.
